Deliber8

Where every argument finds its answer. Structured debate rooms for minds that like to be challenged.

Android iOS
By Am3r Group
What is Deliber8?

Great ideas are
forged in debate.

Deliber8 is a structured debate platform that brings civility and rigor back to online discourse. Whether you're debating ethics, current events, or just want to sharpen your thinking, Deliber8 gives every argument a fair arena.

Move beyond comment sections. Deliber8 creates a space where reasoning matters, evidence counts, and both sides get a fair say.

Key Features

Argue smarter.

Formal & Street Style Debates

The floor is yours when it's your turn — no interruptions, no disrespect. Say your piece, make your case, then let the people decide who's right.

AI Fact Finding

Share your screen with your favourite AI tool and back your argument with real facts — or expose the flaws in your opponent's claims before they even finish their point.

⚖️

Jury of Your Peers

Host or join a debate, step in as a debater or take a seat on the jury. When the arguments are done, the jury Deliber8s — the closest thing to a real debate you'll find on a screen.
